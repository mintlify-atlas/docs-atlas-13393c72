Chromebook Utilities is an open-source reference collection created and maintained by **S-PScripts**, curating hundreds of exploits, bypasses, cheats, proxies, games, and tools specifically for managed or enterprise-enrolled Chromebooks. Whether you are looking to understand ChromeOS system-level vulnerabilities, bypass monitoring extensions like GoGuardian or Securly, find a working proxy or web-based OS, get cheats for classroom quiz platforms, or simply follow tutorials to host your own unblocker, this collection brings it all together in one place. The collection draws on the work of the broader ChromeOS research community — including Titanium Network and Mercury Workshop — and is structured into clearly separated sections so you can find exactly what you need for your ChromeOS version and use case.

<Note>
  This collection is **no longer frequently updated** by S-PScripts due to waning interest. For the most current exploits, proxies, and community discussion, visit [Titanium Network](https://titaniumnetwork.org/) and join their Discord at **.gg/unblock**.
</Note>

## Project Origin & Community

Chromebook Utilities was created by **S-PScripts** as a curated, single-repository reference — similar in spirit to [ByePassHub](https://github.com/wea-f/ByePassHub) by wea-f (a [backup of the original](https://github.com/Space-Fox1/ByePassHub-BACKUP-) is available before wea-f archived theirs). S-PScripts did not author most of the underlying exploits; the collection aggregates and documents work from across the ChromeOS research community.

The two primary organizations behind most of the exploits and tooling referenced in this collection are:

- **Titanium Network** — the largest community for ChromeOS unblockers, proxies, and exploits. Website: [titaniumnetwork.org](https://titaniumnetwork.org/) · Docs: [docs.titaniumnetwork.org](https://docs.titaniumnetwork.org/) · GitHub: [titaniumnetwork-dev](https://github.com/titaniumnetwork-dev) · Discord: **.gg/unblock**
- **Mercury Workshop** — a hardware and software hacking collective focused on ChromeOS and embedded systems. Website: [mercurywork.shop](https://mercurywork.shop/) · GitHub: [MercuryWorkshop](https://github.com/MercuryWorkshop)

If you want live help, updated links, and the latest exploit releases, the **Titanium Network Discord** at **.gg/unblock** is the best place to go.
